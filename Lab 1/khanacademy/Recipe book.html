Ruff soup

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 4 person
Ingredients Quantity
Ruffs 400 g
Water 1 Liter
Carrot 1 pc
Onion 1 pc

Step 1: Prepare the products.

Step 2:Peel the ruffs from the scales, open the abdomen and remove the insides. Wash each fish both inside and out. Put the ruffs in a saucepan or cauldron.

Step 3:Peel the onion and carrot and rinse thoroughly. Chop the onion into four pieces, and cut the carrots into small circles. Add the vegetable slices to the pan with the fish.
